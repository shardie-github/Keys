# Notebook Decision Log Template

Use this template to document decisions during exploratory analysis or data work.

## Problem statement

- What question are we answering?
- What constraints or deadlines apply?

## Data sources

- Source system(s) and access scope
- Data freshness and known limitations

## Assumptions

- List assumptions and why they are reasonable
- Note what would invalidate them

## Decisions

| Decision | Rationale | Alternatives Considered |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Example: Use weekly aggregation | Reduces noise for trend analysis | Daily rollups, rolling 30-day |

## Validation

- How did you validate results?
- What follow-up checks are needed?

## Next steps

- Open questions
- Required follow-up work

## Standard Closing

**Use this closing for all sales conversations:**

```
"KEYS is the keyring to modern tools. We provide proven patterns that unlock 
capability in tools you already own—Cursor, Jupyter, GitHub, Stripe, and more.

The value proposition is clear: proven patterns that save 8-16 hours per pattern, 
cost $79-299 vs. $1,200-2,400 for consultants, and reduce risk with production-tested, 
security-reviewed implementations.

KEYS never replaces your tools. We unlock capability in tools you already use.

What would you like to do next?"
```

**Key Elements:**
- Reinforce brand: "KEYS is the keyring to modern tools"
- Restate value: time savings, cost savings, risk reduction
- Reinforce positioning: unlocks tools, doesn't replace them
- Open question: "What would you like to do next?"

## Closing Principles

### Always Include
- **Brand reinforcement**: "KEYS is the keyring to modern tools"
- **Value restatement**: time savings, cost savings, risk reduction
- **Positioning reinforcement**: unlocks tools, doesn't replace them
- **Clear next step**: specific action item or open question

### Never Include
- **Pressure tactics**: "Limited time offer," "Act now"
- **Hype language**: "Revolutionary," "game-changing"
- **Replacement claims**: "KEYS replaces [Tool]"
- **Vague promises**: "We'll solve everything"

---

## Post-Closing Follow-Up

### If Prospect Is Interested
- Send follow-up email within 24 hours
- Include: marketplace link, specific Key recommendations, next steps
- Schedule follow-up if agreed upon
- Provide additional resources if requested

### If Prospect Is Skeptical
- Send follow-up email acknowledging concerns
- Provide: case studies, technical documentation, specific Key examples
- Leave door open for future conversation
- Don't push if clearly not interested

### If Prospect Needs Time
- Send follow-up email with resources
- Provide: marketplace link, Key catalog, pricing information
- Schedule check-in in 1-2 weeks
- Respect their timeline

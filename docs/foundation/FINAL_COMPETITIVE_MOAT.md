## Core Principle

**KEYS compounds value across pricing, trust, and structure.**

- **Pricing moat**: Value-based pricing that undercuts labor

### 1. Pricing Moat

**Component**: Value-based pricing that undercuts labor

**How It Works**:
- KEYS priced by outcome value, not code volume
- KEYS 10-50x cheaper than consultants
- KEYS 3-40x cheaper than internal development
- KEYS reusable across projects (value compounds)

**Why It Compounds**:
- **Labor Comparison**: KEYS cheaper than any labor alternative
- **Reusability**: Value compounds across projects
- **Risk Reduction**: Prevents costly mistakes
- **Experience Encoding**: Years of experience at fraction of cost

**Competitive Advantage**: Competitors can't compete on price with labor

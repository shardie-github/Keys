The Challenge

Acme Corp was facing a massive sprawl of API keys across 10,000 developer environments. As they scaled their microservices architecture, manual secret rotation was taking weeks, and security compliance audits were becoming a bottleneck for release cycles.

Developers were often hardcoding secrets into configuration files for local testing, leading to accidental commits to version control. The security team lacked visibility into who had access to which production keys.

  • Fragmented secret management across 50+ engineering teams.
  • High risk of credential leakage in CI/CD pipelines.
  • Compliance fines looming due to lack of audit trails.

The Solution

Acme Corp deployed KEYS to centralize secret management. By integrating the KEYS CLI into their developer onboarding script, every engineer gained instant, scoped access to necessary environment variables without ever seeing the raw values.

The implementation included:

check_circle Just-in-Time Access

Secrets are injected only at runtime and revoked immediately after process termination.

check_circle Automated Rotation

Database credentials are automatically rotated every 24 hours without downtime.

The Outcome

Within 90 days, Acme Corp achieved full SOC2 compliance regarding secret management. The "Zero Trust" initiative, which was projected to take 12 months, was completed in 3.

Most importantly, the developer onboarding time—previously a week-long process of requesting access and configuring local .env files—dropped to under 2 hours.

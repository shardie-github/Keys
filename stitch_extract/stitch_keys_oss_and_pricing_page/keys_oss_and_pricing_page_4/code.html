Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really host this myself for free?

Yes! The Community Edition is 100% open source under the MIT license. You can spin it up on your own servers using our Docker image.

What happens if I downgrade from Cloud to Community?

Since both versions use the same underlying engine, you can export your data from the Cloud version and import it into your self-hosted instance at any time.

Do you offer discounts for open source maintainers?

Absolutely. If you maintain a popular open source project, we offer a free tier of our Managed Cloud. Contact us for details.
